Series

Lantern Beach Universe

This is actually a series of series written by Christy Barritt. It started when a cop from the west coast moved to Lantern Beach, a small island off the coast of North Carolina in an unofficial witness protection program. It contains several series where the story continues to build throughout the series and a couple series of standalone novels.

Amelia Peabody Mysteries

Amelia Peabody is a self-proclaimed detective who solves mysteries (mostly murder mysteries) during the excavation season in Egypt, with an occasional mystery in England. The series begins around 1880 and runs through WWI. As the series progresses, her entire family becomes involved in solving the mysteries.
